## About us

We are a team of experienced smart contract researchers, who strive to provide the best smart contract security services possible to DeFi protocols.

Both members of [Egis Security](https://twitter.com/EgisSec) have a proven track record on public auditing platforms such as Code4rena, Sherlock & Codehawks, uncovering more than 90 High/Medium severity vulnerabilities, with ~$40,000 in winnings.
